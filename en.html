THIS WE DO

According to the complexity, our integrated service is offered confidentially in the following 3 consecutive steps to obtain a product on the market:

1 Conceptualization

We will devise a solution product for a need of your area, which connects your small business state with your ideas and goals, taking into account relevant facts of the context.

1-1 Exploration (Included)

Superficial research and study of the context, the market, the segment and your business.

1-2 Research (Recommended)

Detailed research and study of the context, the market, the segment and your business.

2 Develop

We will develop this product, ready to be manufactured in batches with semi-industrial tooling, taking into account its status as a small company.

2-1 Patent (Recommended)

Assistance in the patenting process of your product at INAPI.

2-2 Feedback (Recommended)

Research and detection of weak points in the usability and manufacturing of your product in the growth phase.

3 Batch-manufacturing

We will manufacture this product on a medium scale (up to 5000 un.) and give it visibility in our social networks so you can test it with your customers, get data and justify a future investment.

Design of speacialized tools and simulation of their productive operation.

3-3 Recovering (Recommended)

Purchase of stock of products for reuse as raw material.

HOW WE DO IT

Depending on the requirements of the project, we can perform the following operations to work on different materials:

Tallado 3D

Volume generation (under and over relief) from material removal by chip removal in 3 axes (XYZ).

Aluminum, Plastic, Wood

2D cutting

Generation of flat parts from the removal of perimeter material by chip removal in 2 axes (XY).

Aluminum, Plastic, Wood, Textile, Cardboard

2D engraving

Generation of details under relief by chip removal in 2 (XY) for planes or 3 axes (XYZ) for volumes.

Aluminum, Plastic, Wood

3D Printing

Generation of flat and volumetric parts by addition of material in 2 (XY) or 3 axes (XYZ).

Plastics (PLA, PET)

Laminated

Generation of rigid surfaces of single or double curvature from the gluing of sheets in 2 (XY) or 3 axes (XYZ).

Wood, Cardboard

Bending

Generation of rigid or flexible surfaces (depending on the material) of simple curvature from bending in 2 (XY) or 3 axes (XYZ).

Ferrous, Plastic, Wood
