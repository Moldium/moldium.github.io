This we do

According to the complexity of your challenge, our integrated service is offered confidentially in 3 consecutive steps until, products and packaging, are introduced to the market.

In the following blocks, check what your idea requires:

1 Conceptualization

We will devise a solution product for a need of your area, which connects your small business state with your ideas and goals, taking into account relevant facts of the context.

1-1 Exploration

Superficial research and study of the context, the market, the segment and your business.

1-2 Research

Detailed research and study of the context, the market, the segment and your business.

2 Develop

We will develop this product, ready to be manufactured in batches with semi-industrial tooling, taking into account its status as a small company.

2-1 Patent

Assistance in the patenting process of your product at INAPI (Chile).

2-2 Feedback

Research and detection of weak points in the usability and manufacturing of your product in the growth phase.

3 Batch-manufacturing

We will manufacture this product on a medium scale (up to 5000 un.) and give it visibility in our social networks so you can test it with your customers, get data and justify a future investment.

3-1 Tooling

Design of speacialized tools and simulation of their productive operation.

3-2 Promotion

Promotion on Moldium¬Æ's social networks, including feedback metrics.

3-3 Recovering

Purchase of stock of products for reuse as raw material.
